# Affiliate Program

## 1. What is the Affiliate Program?

The Affiliate Program offers individuals the opportunity to earn commissions by referring new clients through tracked affiliate links.

Enjoy competitive commissions based on your referred clients' advertising spend and start earning rewards today.

## 2. Who can join the Affiliate Program?

The Affiliate Program is open to existing customers who are satisfied with the platform's services and would like to refer new clients.

## 3. What are the benefits of joining the Affiliate Program?

When your referred clients start using the platform's services and spend on advertising, you can earn bonuses based on the applicable reward milestones.

## 4. How does the Affiliate Program work?

### ✅ STEP 1: Share your referral link

On the platform, navigate to the **Affiliate Program** tab.

Click **Copy this link** and share it with your friends, followers, or network.

![Step 1](/img/affiliate/1.png)

Add a referral posted (if you have a website or blog) — embed a visual posted on your social or website to link users directly to your referral URL. (You can skip this step.)

![Step 1 Banner](/img/affiliate/2.png)

### ✅ STEP 2: Referred clients sign up and start using the platform's services

When referred clients complete the registration process through your referral link and begin using the platform's services, you can track their total advertising spend.

![Step 2](/img/affiliate/3.png)

### ✅ STEP 3: Relax and start earning

Referral bonuses can be used to pay for services on the platform or credited to your user balance before the **15th of each month**.
