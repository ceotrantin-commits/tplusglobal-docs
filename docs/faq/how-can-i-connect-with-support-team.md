# How can I connect with support team?

If you have any questions, please contact our support team by:

- Clicking the **Live Chat** icon in the bottom-right corner of the screen.

![Live Chat](/img/faq/support-team/1.png)

- Or selecting **Contact Support** from the **"?"** icon in the top-right corner.

![Contact Support](/img/faq/support-team/2.png)
