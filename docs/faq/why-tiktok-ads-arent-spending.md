# Why TikTok ads aren't spending?

## 1. Ad budget problem

### a. Out of budget

TikTok advertisers often face the problem of ads exceeding budget limits. The minimum campaign budget is **$50**, with ad groups starting at **$20**.

**Solution:**

- Ensure payment goes through to prevent ads from not delivering.
- The Platform suggests initial budget to cover at least **(50 × targeted CPA)**.

### b. Low bidding

To ensure optimal ad placement, it's crucial to balance your bidding price with the market. TikTok's bidding methods allow users to bid for ad placements, with higher bids resulting in better placements. If your bidding is too low, your ad will be placed beneath your competitors, making it less likely to be seen.

**Solution:** To ensure your bidding strategy stays in line with the market, adjust your bids according to the bidding methods and your competitors. If you're new to running ads on TikTok, consider using the **Lowest Cost** bidding strategy.

---

## 2. Incorrect targeting

### a. Overlapping targeting

Using too many targeting criteria can negatively impact the TikTok ads distribution algorithm, making it difficult for TikTok to allocate your ads to the right audience.

**Solution:** Choose the target ad group's criteria and characteristics carefully, avoiding overly specific features that may lead to targeting too deeply.

### b. Too in-depth targeting

Overly specific targeting can limit the reach of your TikTok ads, resulting in poor visibility and less interaction.

**Solution:** Expand your audience range by including potential customers or **Lookalike Audiences** in the Ad Manager settings.

### c. Too large targeting

Targeting too broadly may cause TikTok to show your ads to users who are not interested in your product or service, leading to poor ad delivery.

**Solution:** Optimize your target audience by considering their gender, age, interests, and the topics they often watch on TikTok. Be careful not to choose too many characteristics to avoid overlapping targeting. Then, let TikTok's algorithm optimize the audience accordingly.

---

## 3. Ad creatives problem

### a. Policy violation

If your ad violates TikTok's policy — such as the ad caption not being consistent with the corresponding ad image or video — it will result in your ad not spending.

**Solution:** Ensure that your advertising is in line with TikTok's **Ad Creative Policy**.

### b. Poor Ad creative

One of the most common mistakes is either reposting content from other platforms or using outdated content for advertising. These types of content often fail to engage users since TikTok's distribution algorithm prioritizes users' experiences.

**Solution:** To create successful TikTok ads, embrace the platform's spontaneity, creativity, and fun. We suggest following these three keynotes:

- Adapt to **UGC (user-generated content)** to create ads relevant to viewers.
- Include **KOL, KOC, and influencers** in your ads to attract users.
- Keep up with the latest trends, including soundtracks, filters, and hashtags.

---

## 4. Account problem

TikTok reviews all accounts before issuing them to ensure compliance with Advertising Policies. This process can take up to **24 hours**, during which campaigns and ads will not be delivered.

**Solution:** While your account is under review, you cannot create an ad campaign on TikTok. Advertisers must wait until the review is complete before adding ad creatives.

If your account is denied, TikTok will provide feedback on the missing or violated criteria. However, creating a new account is the only solution for penalized accounts to avoid policy violations.
