# Ad Account Revocation

Guide to understanding the Ad Account Revocation policy and its impact on your Ad Accounts.

- If there is no advertising spend within **5 days** from the ad account creation date, the Platform reserves the right to revoke the ad account.

- If an ad account is revoked:

  - Your administrator's access to the ad account will be removed.
  - You will not be able to add funds to or withdraw funds from the ad account.
  - The Platform retains full discretion to enforce this policy in accordance with its internal risk management procedures and platform compliance requirements.

- If you need support or want to request access to a revoked ad account, please contact our support team.
