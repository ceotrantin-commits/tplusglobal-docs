# Customer Tier

Your total spending determines the number of ad accounts you can create. Each tier defines the maximum ad account limit per customer, as shown in the table below.

| Tier | | Steel | Bronze | Silver | Gold | Platinum | Diamond |
|------|------|:------:|:------:|:------:|:------:|:---------:|:--------:|
| **Condition** | Total Ad spending | Default | $1,000 | $10,000 | $50,000 | $200,000 | $1,000,000 |
| **Benefit** | Maximum number of Ad Accounts | 2 | 3 | 5 | 10 | 20 | 100 |
