# Partnership Program

Explore the opportunity to forge a partnership with the Platform by becoming a Sub-Agency or a Country Manager, which entails benefits for both partners and their clients.

## 1. What is the Partnership Program?

The Partnership Program offers a unique opportunity for both the Platform and its partners to expand their businesses in the advertising industry. As a leading TikTok Marketing Partner, the Platform provides resources, training, guidance, and best practices for other agencies, creating a win-win situation.

## 2. Why become a Partner?

As TikTok becomes a highly sought-after opportunity, particularly for advertising agencies seeking effective advertising solutions for their clients, not every agency can effectively provide solutions and manage clients without the right resources and guidance. That's where the Platform steps in.

By partnering with us, agencies can access the tools, knowledge, and best practices needed to drive productive outcomes for their clients.

- **Effective management:** The Platform provides its partners with an advanced platform that helps agencies track business performance, manage clients, and ensure operational and financial transparency.
- **Increased benefits:** Leveraging the Platform's resources and benefits, partners can maximize client potential, refine their strategies, and scale their businesses.
- **Flexibility:** The Platform offers tailored partnership programs to each partner, ensuring that both partners and their clients receive the right benefits for their needs.
- **Business expansion:** With the Platform's resources and support, partners can scale their businesses more easily and effectively.
- **Simplicity:** The Platform's streamlined working processes save time and costs for both partners and their clients.

## 3. What is included in our Partnership Package?

As a TikTok agency with a proven track record of success, we possess a deep understanding of what contributes to agency success. With this knowledge in mind, our Partnership Package is designed to offer mutual benefits for agencies or our partners and their clients, ensuring effective advertising on TikTok.

| Agency's Benefits | Client's Benefits |
| --- | --- |
| Access to a well-established infrastructure to save time and effort. | A simplified and effective advertising experience. |
| An advanced technical platform for better client management. | Tailor-made benefits in terms of ad accounts, minimum top-up requirements, taxes, and payment management. |
| Attractive, customized commission rates based on monthly ad spending. | Exclusive access to new features, beta testing opportunities, and pilot programs. |
| Integration with other partner systems to create a unified, seamless experience for your clients. | Unique opportunities to join booster, cashback, and ad credit programs. |

## 4. Who can be a Partner?

We are actively seeking partnerships with agencies specializing in Digital Marketing or TikTok advertising. We believe in creating tailor-made partnership proposals for each of our potential Partners. However, there are several key attributes that we look for, which include:

- Dedicated support teams for TikTok advertising training and consultation.
- Extensive advertising expertise across social media platforms.
- Ethical and sustainable business practices that contribute to long-term success.
- Well-developed local industry networks and strong market influence.

## 5. How to become a Partner?

- **Step 1: Register**

Complete the registration form here, and our team will reach out to you within 1–2 business days via Email or WhatsApp.

- **Step 2: Evaluation**

Upon receiving your information, our team will assess the potential for a partnership based on your business model, clientele, and industry influence.

- **Step 3: Customize & Negotiate**

Based on the information you provide, we will propose a partnership program with benefits tailored to your needs and the needs of your clients.

- **Step 4: Launch & Progress**

Once a mutual agreement is reached, we will provide the necessary resources and materials, such as a technical management platform, marketing materials, and learning resources, enabling partners to begin their journey.
