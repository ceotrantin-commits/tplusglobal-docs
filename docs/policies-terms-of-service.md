## 4. Autopayment Policy

This policy aims to provide specific and clear information regarding the use of the Auto Topup feature on the TPlus Global platform. By accepting this policy, users allow the system to automatically transfer funds from their TPlus Global account to the Ads account when a predetermined threshold is reached.

### Conditions and Terms

#### Confirmation and Acceptance

By using the Auto Topup feature on the TPlus Global platform, users must agree to the terms and conditions of this policy. Utilizing this feature implies the user's acceptance of the system automatically executing payment transactions according to their settings.

#### Threshold Setting

Users can set a specific threshold for their Ads account balance. When the Ads account balance reaches or falls below this threshold, the system will automatically top up the Ads account from the TPlus Global balance.

#### Top-up Amount Setting

Users can set the desired amount to top up their Ads account.

#### Functionality and Flexibility

The Auto Topup feature is designed to provide flexibility for users in managing advertising expenses. Users can turn off or adjust the settings of this feature at any time from their account on the TPlus Global platform.

#### Privacy and Security Policy

All payment transactions executed through the Auto Topup feature will adhere to the security and privacy regulations of the TPlus Global platform. User account and payment information will be protected in accordance with our Privacy Policy.
