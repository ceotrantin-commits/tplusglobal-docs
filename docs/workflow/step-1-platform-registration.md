# Step 1: Platform Account Registration

Guide to registering a Platform account.

---

## 1. Register with Email

- Access the TPlus Global platform or referral link.

- Click **Register**.

<img src="/img/workflow/step-1/1.png" width="850" />

- Fill in the required information:

  - Full Name
  - Email Address
  - Phone Number (WhatsApp / Telegram)
  - Password
  - Confirm Password

- Accept the Terms of Service.

- Click **Register**.

<img src="/img/workflow/step-1/2.png" width="850" />

- Check your email and enter the verification code.

<img src="/img/workflow/step-1/3.png" width="850" />

- Your account has been created successfully.

<img src="/img/workflow/step-1/4.png" width="850" />

---

## 2. Register with Google Account

- Access the TPlus Global platform or referral link.

- Click **Continue with Google**.

<img src="/img/workflow/step-1/5.png" width="850" />

- Select your Google account.

<img src="/img/workflow/step-1/6.png" width="850" />

- Click **Continue** to authorize.

<img src="/img/workflow/step-1/7.png" width="850" />

- Your account has been created successfully.

<img src="/img/workflow/step-1/8.png" width="850" />

---

## 3. Register with Code

- Access the TPlus Global platform or referral link.

- Click **Register with Code**.

<img src="/img/workflow/step-1/9.png" width="850" />

- Enter your email address.

- Click **Send Verification Code**.

<img src="/img/workflow/step-1/10.png" width="850" />

- Check your email and enter the verification code.

<img src="/img/workflow/step-1/11.png" width="850" />

- Click **Verify**.

- Your account has been created successfully.

<img src="/img/workflow/step-1/12.png" width="850" />
