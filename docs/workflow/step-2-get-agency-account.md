# Step 2: Get TikTok Agency Ad Account

Guide to Getting a TikTok Agency Ad Account.

Once you have successfully registered a Platform account, you can proceed to obtain a TikTok Agency Ad Account. The following steps will guide you through the process of creating and setting up your Ad Account.

---

## 1. Connect TikTok for Business Account

- Click the button **Connect** to connect with your TikTok for Business. (If you don't have any TikTok for Business, you can create one by clicking **Create now**.)

<img src="/img/workflow/step-2/1.png" width="850" />

- Enter the email and password of your **TikTok for Business** account and click **Log in**.

<img src="/img/workflow/step-2/2.png" width="850" />

- Agree to terms and permissions related to your TikTok ad account.

- Click **Confirm**.

<img src="/img/workflow/step-2/3.png" width="850" />

- Successful connection.

- After successfully connecting, the system automatically proceeds to the next step.

---

## 2. Connect TikTok for Business Center

- Select an existing BC to connect

<img src="/img/workflow/step-2/4.png" width="850" />

- Click **Create New** to create a new account.

<img src="/img/workflow/step-2/5.png" width="850" />

- Fill in the account information:

  - Business Center name
  - Country or region
  - Contact information – Your Phone Number, Your Secondary Industry, Your Business Name, Company Website

- Click **Create Now**.

<img src="/img/workflow/step-2/6.png" width="850" />


- **NOTE**
>
> If the TikTok for Business account has 3 or more Business Centers, you cannot create a new Business Center. Please select one of the existing Business Centers.


>
- After successfully connecting, the system automatically proceeds to the next step.

---
## 3. Create TikTok Ad Account


- Fill in the Business information:

  - Click on the dropdown on **Business Center Name** to select Business Center
  - Enter your **Ad Account Name**
  - Click on the dropdown on **Time Zone** to select a Time Zone
  - Your Business Name
  - Company Website
  

- Click on the button **Create new**

<img src="/img/workflow/step-2/7.png" width="850" />

- Your ad account has been created successfully!


<img src="/img/workflow/step-2/8.png" width="850" />
