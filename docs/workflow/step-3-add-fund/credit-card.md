# Credit Card

Payment process via Credit Card.

- Select the **Add Balance** button and select **Credit Card** as your payment method.

- Supported cards include Visa, Mastercard, American Express (Amex), Discover, and other eligible card types.

<img src="/img/workflow/step-3-add-fund/credit-card/1.png" width="850" />

---

## 1. Add Credit Card

- If no card has been added yet, click **Add Credit Card** to add a new payment method.

<img src="/img/workflow/step-3-add-fund/credit-card/2.png" width="850" />

- Fill in information:

  - Email
  - Card Number
  - Expired
  - CVC
  - Name on Card

- Click the button **Add Card**.

<img src="/img/workflow/step-3-add-fund/credit-card/3.png" width="850" />

- After adding a credit card, its status may appear as **Pending** until verification is completed. To activate the card, click **Activate Now**.

<img src="/img/workflow/step-3-add-fund/credit-card/4.png" width="850" />

- A Card Activation popup will appear, click **Get Code**.

- Check your bank transaction history, SMS messages, or email inbox for the activation code. The activation code will be in the format **VERIFYCODE*XXXX**.

- Enter the **XXXX** code in the **OTP** field.

- Click **Activate**.

<img src="/img/workflow/step-3-add-fund/credit-card/5.png" width="850" />

- A confirmation popup will appear, click **Yes** to continue.

<img src="/img/workflow/step-3-add-fund/credit-card/6.png" width="850" />

- Once verification is successful, a success message will be displayed, click **Close**.

<img src="/img/workflow/step-3-add-fund/credit-card/7.png" width="850" />

- The card status will then change to **Verified**.

<img src="/img/workflow/step-3-add-fund/credit-card/8.png" width="850" />

---

## 2. Add balance by Credit Card

- Select the credit card you want to use.

- Enter the amount and click **Top up**.

<img src="/img/workflow/step-3-add-fund/credit-card/9.png" width="850" />

---

## 3. Remove your Credit Card

- If you want to remove the credit card from our platform, click the icon **Delete**.

<img src="/img/workflow/step-3-add-fund/credit-card/10.png" width="850" />

- Click **OK**.

<img src="/img/workflow/step-3-add-fund/credit-card/11.png" width="850" />
