# Payoneer

Payment process via Payoneer.

- Click the **Add Balance** button and select **Payoneer** as your payment method.

- Enter the amount you want to add to your account, then click **Continue**.

<img src="/img/workflow/step-3-add-fund/payoneer/1.png" width="850" />

- Transfer an amount equal to the **Total Amount** shown in the **Summary** section to the displayed **Payoneer Receiving Account**.

- After completing the transfer, enter the **Transaction ID (TxID)**.

- Upload the **billing receipt**.

- Click **Done**.

<img src="/img/workflow/step-3-add-fund/payoneer/2.png" width="850" />

- We will review your payment. If everything is valid, the amount will be added to your account.

<img src="/img/workflow/step-3-add-fund/payoneer/3.png" width="850" />
