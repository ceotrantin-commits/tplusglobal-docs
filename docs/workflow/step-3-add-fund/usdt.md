# USDT

Payment process via USDT.

- Click the **Add Balance** button and select **USDT** as your payment method.

<img src="/img/workflow/step-3-add-fund/usdt/1.png" width="850" />

- Select **Coin** — Choose the coin you want to use.
<img src="/img/workflow/step-3-add-fund/usdt/2.png" width="850" />

- Select **Transfer Network** — Select the correct network.

- Enter **Amount** — Choose the amount you want to add to the balance.

- Click the **Continue** button.


- Transfer the money from your wallet to our wallet address. Please make sure to follow the information exactly:

  - Use the exact **coin** you selected.
  - Use the exact **network** you selected.
  - Copy the exact **wallet address**.
  - Copy the exact **amount** shown (**DO NOT** round it).

- Once you complete the transaction:

  - Enter the exact **Transaction ID**.
  - Upload a screenshot of the billing/transfer as an attachment.

- Click **Done**.

<img src="/img/workflow/step-3-add-fund/usdt/3.png" width="850" />

- You've successfully added funds. Please check your account balance.

:::note

If you encounter any issues, please contact your Account Manager directly for support.

:::
