# Step 4: Money Allocation

Guide to transferring funds from your Wallet to an Ad Account.

## 1. Transfer money from Wallet account to the ad account

- Click **Payment** => click **Add Fund** in the ad account you want to add funds to.

<img src="/img/workflow/step-4/1.png" width="850" />

- Check the correct **Ad Account Name**, **Ad Account ID**.

- Enter the amount you want to add in the **Transfer Amount** box.

- Click **Apply**.

<img src="/img/workflow/step-4/2.png" width="850" />

- After adding funds from Wallet to the Ad Account successfully:

  - You will receive an email notifying you of the successful Add Funds to the Ad Account.
  - You can check the transaction in the **Ad Account Transaction History** under the **Payment** tab.

<img src="/img/workflow/step-4/3.png" width="850" />

## 2. Withdraw money from ad account to TPlus Global account

- Click **Payment** => click **Withdraw** in the ad account you want.

- Check the correct **Ad Account Name**, **Ad Account ID**.

- Enter the amount you want to withdraw.

- Click **Apply**.
